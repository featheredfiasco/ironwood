About IronWood

Our Story

Built from real-world experience.

IronWood Property Repair grew out of years of experience in customer service and property management. We saw firsthand how important it is to have reliable, skilled professionals who can handle property repairs and maintenance efficiently — and how rare that combination actually is.

Our background taught us what property owners and managers genuinely need: clear communication, dependable service, and quality work that lasts. We understand the importance of getting things done right the first time and keeping projects on schedule.

We built IronWood on the principle of doing the job right. That means using proper techniques, quality materials, and taking the time to ensure every repair meets our standards. It also means being responsive, showing up when scheduled, and standing behind our work.

Meet the Owner

Ples Walker

Ples founded IronWood Property Repair with a clear vision: to provide property owners and managers with reliable, high-quality repair and maintenance services they can trust. With an extensive background in customer service and property management, Ples understands the unique challenges and demands of keeping properties in excellent condition.

Throughout his career in property management, Ples developed a deep appreciation for the importance of responsive, professional service. That experience revealed what property owners genuinely value: clear communication, dependable service, and quality work. He built IronWood Property Repair around these core principles.

When you work with IronWood, you're working directly with someone who cares deeply about your property and your satisfaction. Ples's hands-on approach ensures that every project reflects the high standards and personal integrity that define the business.

Vacation Rental Specialists

Trusted partner for vacation rental management.

Drawing on Ples Walker's deep roots in property management, IronWood understands the fast-paced, high-stakes environment of vacation rental management. Protecting your investment and securing five-star reviews requires a flawless guest experience — which relies heavily on meticulous, timely short-term rental maintenance.

Rapid property turnover & repairs

We specialize in high-quality rental property repairs designed to facilitate seamless property turnover. Whether it's knocking out a detailed punch list between tight guest transitions or handling emergency vacation rental maintenance calls, we provide the reliability and lightning-fast response times property managers need.

Proactive STR maintenance

Beyond emergency fixes, we act as a strategic partner for short-term rental owners by implementing comprehensive, preventative STR maintenance. Addressing minor issues before they disrupt a guest's stay keeps your property booked, your reviews high, and your peace of mind intact.

  • Quick guest transitions
  • Turnover punch lists
  • Preventative care plans
  • Responsive communication
